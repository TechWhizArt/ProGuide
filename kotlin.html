Kotlin

Overview

Kotlin is a modern, statically-typed programming language developed by JetBrains, designed for cross-platform development. It's fully interoperable with Java and widely used for Android app development. Known for its concise syntax, safety features, and enhanced productivity, Kotlin supports object-oriented and functional programming paradigms, making it a popular choice among developers.
